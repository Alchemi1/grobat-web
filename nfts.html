SACRED MOTHER

The Sacred Mother, Olivia the Petricious, went through stages of being ignored by her own mother. She frequently left her alone both emotionally and physically. With only darkness to talk to, she sought conversation in her crystal ball or turned to her diary to scribble make-believe stories. Despite keeping herself out of the way, Olivia was often screamed at and called 'ungrateful' for the smallest of things. "I'm better now, it's true, but the damage that has been done, can never be undone. It's still there, itching quietly beneath the surface, like a sharp metal wire cutting into me for too long" Olivia said. One day she escaped death and ran away with the darkness that was her only company for so many years. Hidden inside a deep cave, Olivia was surrounded by 10,000 bats. Whilst crying out, she cast a horrible spell on the world that had turned its back on her... "Grrrrooooobats" she whispered. The crystal ball emitted a distant glow that turned into the longest night, causing the eternal darkness that started the Grobats era
