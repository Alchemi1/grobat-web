So, what exactly is an NFT?

An NFT is a Non-fungible Token that exists as a smart contract on a blockchain. Each NFT is unique in its own right, and they can be owned by people who hold them in a smart wallet. What is an auto-staking NFT and what makes this SO SPECIAL? Auto staking NFTs traded on the marketplace leverage the royalties from those secondary sales to generate yield, which gets split between the original minter, the NFT holders, and the marketplace fees. The NFTs do not need to be locked away in a staking contract, saving both token fees and gas fees. Just from holding any Hype auto staking NFT, you can generate a passive income for as long as it is in your wallet. NFTs in the marketplace, will not receive dividends for as long as it is in the marketplace, instead, those dividends will be distributed to the holders who are not selling their NFT, compounding the yield to dedicated holders.
